About Me

That resourcefulness shaped how I approach challenges in today. I’ve always been drawn to puzzles and strategy, which probably explains why games like The Legend of Zelda, Halo Wars, and Civilization have always fascinated me. Much like navigating dungeons, leading armies, or building empires, coding is about strategy and finding the smartest path forward. Every project—whether it’s developing a web scraper, creating a game, or optimizing an algorithm—feels like solving the ultimate puzzle. Technology excites me, and I love diving into new tools and breakthroughs. Outside of programming, I have a strong interest in trading stocks, exploring cryptocurrency, and even collecting crypto cards. It’s a world where strategy and timing meet innovation, which resonates with my problem-solving mindset. As a fresh graduate, I’m eager to apply my skills and ingenuity to create impactful solutions while continuing to grow both personally and professionally.
